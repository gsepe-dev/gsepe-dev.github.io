NEXTCLOUD: THE ULTIMATE SELF-HOSTED CLOUD SOLUTION

  • Open Source

In the current landscape of cloud productivity suites, Nextcloud stands out as one of the most interesting alternatives for those who want control, privacy and customisation. But what makes it truly special compared to the classic Google Workspace and Office 365?

Nextcloud is an open-source cloud storage platform that allows you to easily create a collaborative environment on your own server. This means that your data remains completely under your control, without having to rely on third parties. In fact, privacy and security are the pillars on which Nextcloud bases its offering.

Installation is straightforward even for users who are less familiar with technology, as in addition to the numerous official guides, it is possible to install all-in-one versions, which will manage the entire process autonomously. Once installation is complete, the user interface is clean and intuitive, similar to that of the most popular commercial cloud services, making the transition very smooth. The interface is available in multiple languages, including Italian, and offers a range of features such as file sharing, cloud storage, and cloud backup.

Another strength of Nextcloud is its wide range of features. In addition to simple file storage, it includes applications for contact management, calendars, instant messaging, file sharing and much more. Furthermore, thanks to its active community of developers, you can also install third-party plugins for every need.

In terms of performance, Nextcloud performs excellently. File uploads and downloads are fast and smooth, even with large files. Synchronisation between different devices is seamless and lag-free, allowing you to always have everything you need at your fingertips.

However, Nextcloud is not without its challenges. For example, the maintenance and security of the server itself requires constant effort to ensure that data is always protected from new vulnerabilities that are discovered every day.

In conclusion, Nextcloud is an excellent solution for those seeking a self-hosted alternative to commercial cloud services, prioritising privacy, control and customisation. Despite the challenges it faces, the benefits in terms of security and flexibility make Nextcloud a winning choice for both general users and businesses of all sizes.
